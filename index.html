Audioshelfie is a beautiful, open-source mobile app for streaming and downloading audiobooks from your Audiobookshelf server.

View on GitHub Get Started

Built for audiobook lovers

Everything you need for a great listening experience, nothing you don't.

Stream or Download

Stream audiobooks directly from your server, or download them for offline listening on the go.

Synced Progress

Your listening position syncs automatically with Audiobookshelf. Pick up right where you left off on any device.

Beautiful Library

Browse your entire collection with a clean, cover-art-forward interface designed for discovery.

Sleep Timer

Fall asleep listening with a configurable sleep timer that fades out and bookmarks your position.

Playback Controls

Adjustable playback speed, chapter navigation, skip intervals, and bookmarks for total control.

Cross-Platform

Built with Flutter for a native experience on both iOS and Android from a single codebase.

Get started in minutes

1

Set up Audiobookshelf

Self-host Audiobookshelf on your server and add your audiobook collection.

2

Install Audioshelfie

Download from the App Store, Google Play, or build from source on GitHub.

3

Connect & Listen

Enter your server URL, sign in, and start listening to your library.

Ready to start listening?
